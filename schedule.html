Schedule Page

This is the Schedule Page for my classes. Currently I am in 17 credit hours. You can also find future tasks of mine below.

SPAN 105

SPAN 105: Spanish for High Beginners

Introduction to the Spanish language and a development of speaking, listening, reading and writing skills through the use of basic texts. Currently learning a language through zoom is proving to be a challenge.

COMP 211

COMP 211: Systems Fundamentals

Students enter the course having taken an introductory programming course in a high-level programming language and a course in discrete structures. The overarching goal is to bridge the gap between a students' knowledge of a high-level programming language and computer organization. This class feels like engineering.

HIST 127

HIST 127: American History to 1865

A survey of various aspects of American development during the colonial, revolutionary, and national periods, with stress upon major themes and interpretations. This class teaches me so much about American History.

INLS 161

INLS 161: Tools for Information Literacy

Tools and concepts for information literacy. Includes software use and maintenance, computer applications, and networked information systems. I truly love this class and I made this website for one of my assingments.
